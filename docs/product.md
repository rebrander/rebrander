# Product Concept

Rebrander changes the identity of a project while preserving its architecture and behavior.

## Inputs

- Source GitHub repository.
- Source GitLab repository.
- Source GitHub organization.
- Source GitLab group.
- Target repository or organization path.

## Outputs

- Rebranded repository or repositories.
- Change report.
- Infrastructure checklist.
- Secret setup checklist.
- Validation report.
- Provenance report when needed.

## Modes

### Safe

Only high-confidence mappings are applied. Everything else becomes a review item.

### Reviewed

Medium-risk mappings require user confirmation.

### Aggressive

Broad rewrite for owned projects. This mode is not appropriate for unclear third-party repositories or hosted SaaS jobs without proof of authorization.

### The Core Statement

Rebrander is a tool for the automated rebranding of projects within your own space.

### Use Cases – When to use it?

White-Labeling: Turn generic or base applications into customized, client-ready products with their own unique branding on your machine.

Fork Refactoring: When forking a repository or adopting an open-source project, instantly give it a fresh identity that perfectly matches your ecosystem.

Market Testing: Deploy the exact same functional software under different names to test different audience responses or niches.

Local Cleanup: Clean up legacy names, confusing forks, or temporary project codenames across your entire environment effortlessly.

### Security & Peace of Mind

🔒 100% Local & Private: Everything happens locally within your secure space. Your source code never leaves your machine, and no external servers ever see your data.

🛡️ Non-Invasive Transformation: Rebrander strictly targets names, labels, and visible strings. It is smart enough not to break your database structures, core dependencies, or system logic.

⏪ Full Safety Net (One-Click Rollback): Made a typo? Want to change it back? No worries! Rebrander automatically backs up your state locally, allowing you to undo any transformation instantly.

### Benefits – What’s in it for you?

🚀 Ultimate Time Saver: No more manual text replacements, broken references, or renaming nightmares. Rebrander does the heavy lifting in seconds, right where you work.

🎨 Instant IP Customization: Easily tailor open-source or internal projects for specific clients, markets, or your own team without touching the core logic.

🔄 Total Architectural Control: Your code, logic, and underlying architecture remain 100% untouched and functional. Only the surface changes.

📂 Personalized Workspace Organization: Keep your private environment tidy with clear, distinct project names that fit your personal naming conventions.
